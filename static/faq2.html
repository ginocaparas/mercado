Who are We

Accounts and Registration 


Do I need to register first to buy from Galleon Mercado?
You may buy  from Galleon Mercado as a Guest or you may register as a Buyer after placing an order. 
Please make sure that the mobile number and email address you used upon checkout is the one you will use upon registration.

How to register as a Buyer?
  1. To register an account:
  2. Go to our Galleon Mercado website at http://mercado.galleon.ph/home
  3. Click on the icon at the upper right side of the page
  4. Click "Sign Up Here"
  5. Please enter your Full Name, Mobile Number and Password
  6. Then Click on "Sign Up" (For verification purposes, we send a 4 digit code to the registered mobile number)
  7. Type in the 4 digit verification code we sent on to your registered mobile number and click on "Submit" to register
  8. You will be redirected to the Login Page
  9. Now type in your Registered Mobile Number and Password to start Shopping.
What to do if I forget my password?
  1. Go to our Galleon Mercado website at http://mercado.galleon.ph/home
  2. Click on the icon at the upper right side of the page
  3. Click on "Forget Password"
  4. Enter your registered Mobile Number
  5. We'll send you a 4 digit verification code on to your registered mobile number and type in the code and click on "Submit"
  6. You may now type in your new password and confirm it.
NOTE: If an error still exist please report by contacting our Customer Service Team via FB live chat: https://m.me/galleonmercado  or you may send an email to support.mercado@galleon.ph

What to do if my account got deactivated?
Please report by contacting our Customer Service Team via FB live chat: https://m.me/galleonmercado  or you may send an email to support.mercado@galleon.ph if your account got deactivated.

Orders


How to buy from Galleon Mercado?

  1. Go to our Galleon Mercado website at http://mercado.galleon.ph/home
  2. Browse categories/sub-categories or use product search to find product you want to buy
  3. On the product page, click on "Add to Cart" for the product you wish to buy. Please make sure to check the product detail page for variation. You may choose or indicate preferred; quantity, color, size, etc. You may also type in the promo code.
  4. Once you click on “Add to Cart”,, you will be redirected to the “Shopping Cart”.
  5. Select the products you want to buy from your Shopping Cart and click ”Proceed to “Checkout” Out
  6. On the Check Out page, type in your personal information: Full Name, Mobile Number, Email Address, Billing Address, Shipping Address
  7. Please check product summary. Note: You may click on "Change my Cart" if you want to go back to the "Shopping Cart" if there will be changes on your order.
  8. Choose your Payment with various payment service providers to process your order.
  9. Lastly, you must check the box that shows agreement and consent to the Galleon Mercado Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy, before you click on "Submit Order

Can I Checkout Multiple Products From Different Sellers In My Cart At Once?
Yes, you may add multiple products from different Sellers in your cart at once.
  1. On the Search Bar under "My Cart" you may either type in the Store where you want to shop or type in the name of the product you want to add in your cart.
  2. Then click on the "Cart Button" to review the products you have in your cart,
  3. Click on "Proceed Checkout" once you are done shopping.

Why is Cash Upon Delivery (COD) not acceptable as a payment option?
Cash Upon Delivery is not available for all transactions for Buyers protection. Galleon Mercado secures Buyers by holding money until the tracking shows that your order is delivered, this way Sellers are not able to scam Buyers. Also, Sellers ship items through their preferred Delivery Partners which we do not have control in Cash Upon Delivery (COD). 

We are encouraging Buyers to make payments using these convenient and secured Payment Methods

How to choose a Shipping Option for my order shipment? 
Shipping options are based on the option/s set by the Seller, thus if there's none, Buyers should contact the Seller directly to discuss it with them.   

How to track orders from Galleon Mercado?
  1. Visit http://mercado.galleon.ph/home
  2. At the top-right corner, click on "Track my Order"
  3. Type in your "Order No" and the "Cellphone No." you used upon registration or checkout
  4. Click on "Submit"
What should I do if I haven't received my order yet?
We highly recommend that you constantly check and track the status of your orders through the Galleon Mercado Website from time to time.
  1. Buyers can also check with the Delivery Partners set by the Seller. Sellers on the other hand should provide details such as Name of delivery company, the tracking number, etc through the website.
  2. Buyers must contact the Seller to make a follow up and Sellers must respond to the Buyer soon as possible, and
  3. If there is no response from the Seller within 3 business days, Buyer(s) can request for a return/refund by contacting Galleon Mercado Customer Service Team. Galleon Mercado customer service will respond to the Buyer within 2 business days with the resolution.
What to do if my order is lost, damaged or failed to be delivered?
In the event the item the Buyer purchased is damaged, lost or failed to be delivered:
  1. Buyers must contact the Seller directly to report and Seller must provide a possible resolution within 1-3 business days. Galleon Mercado on the other hand will hold the Buyer’s payment not until the Seller provides a resolution.
  2. If there is no response from the Seller after 3 business days, Buyer(s) can request for a return/refund by contacting Galleon Mercado Customer Service Team. Galleon Mercado customer service will respond to the Buyer within 2 business days with the resolution.
Refer to this link: https://mercado.galleon.ph/document/terms-and-conditions for Galleon Mercado’s Terms and Conditions about returns/replacements/refund.

How can I arrange an order pick up/drop off?
Buyers can contact the Seller directly if order pick up/drop off is possible. 

Does Gallen Mercado have Same-Day Delivery?
Seller(s) can immediately prepare delivery on the same day/next day once a Buyer’s payment for the order has been remitted and verified, but again  Buyers must contact the Seller directly to discuss their delivery options and cost if it is not set.

Cancellations, Returns and Refunds


Cancellations


When and how can a Buyer cancel an order?
Orders can only be cancelled when the items are not yet handed over to the Delivery Partners set by Seller. Buyer must contact the Seller directly to request for cancellation of an order.

Refer to this link: https://mercado.galleon.ph/document/terms-and-conditions for Galleon Mercado’s Terms and Conditions about returns/replacements/refund.


Returns and Replacement


How can a Buyer return an item or request for a replacement?
Orders can be returned if Seller agrees to the return of the item. Buyer may request a return or replacement by contacting the Seller directly. Shipping costs in returning the item should be agreed upon by Buyer and Seller. Shipping cost for the delivery of any replacement should be agreed upon by Buyer and Seller. 

 Buyer may return an order when the following conditions are met:
  • Damaged Item
  • Defective Item
  • Wrong item delivered
  • Missing items/parts
  • Does not fit
  • Used items
  • Items not as advertised
  • Item may not be authentic
  • Expired product
 The following may not be returned or replaced depending on Seller:
  • Goods that are perishable or may otherwise deteriorate or expire rapidly, such as groceries;
  • Goods that are not suitable for return due to health protection or hygienic reasons, such as cosmetics, swimwear and underwear.
Refer to this link: https://mercado.galleon.ph/document/terms-and-conditions for Galleon Mercado’s Terms and Conditions about return/replacement/refund.


Disputes and Refunds


How can a Buyer raise a dispute to Galleon Mercado?
In the event a problem arises in a transaction, the Buyer and Seller agree to communicate with each other first to attempt to resolve such dispute by mutual discussions. If the dispute cannot be resolved by mutual discussions after three (3) business days, Buyers may contact Galleon Mercado to arbitrate and provide a resolution within two (2) business days.

Refer to this link: https://mercado.galleon.ph/document/terms-and-conditions for Galleon Mercado’s Terms and Conditions.

How to get a refund through Galleon Mercado?
Galleon Mercado will provide refunds to Buyers via Bank transfer for Cancellations and Disputes. Galleon Mercado shall have no liability for refunds offered by Seller in excess of the purchase price.

 Refunds is initiated when the following conditions are met:
  1. Order is cancelled by Seller
  2. Order is cancelled by Buyer and Seller approves
  3. Products not delivered by seller
  4. Disputes in favor of Buyer
 *Important Reminders:
  1. Return/Refunds due to change of mind will not be accepted.
  2. Please note that it depends on the type of product. Return of personal items such as undergarments, innergarments, perishable goods and grocery items, and adult products is prohibited.
  3. Buyers can only request for a return/refund once the dispute has been approved by Galleon Mercado.
How long does it take to process a refund?
Once a return/refund is approved by Galleon Mercado, Buyers will be notified through email and SMS. Refund process usually takes 8-10 business days.

  1. Provide us your Full Name, Phone number you used upon placing the order and the Order No.
  2. Give us a detailed reason why you request for return/refund.
