How to Buy

  1. Go to our Galleon Mercado website at http://mercado.galleon.ph/home
  2. Browse categories/sub-categories or use product search to find product you want to buy
  3. On the product page, click on "Add to Cart" for the product you wish to buy. Note: Please make sure to check the product detail page for variation. You may choose or indicate preferred; quantity, color, size, etc. You may also type in the promo code.
  4. Once, confirmed, you will be directed to the Shopping Cart.
  5. Select the products you want to buy from your Shopping Cart and click Check Out.
  6. On the Check Out page, type in your personal information: Full Name, Mobile Number, Email Address, Billing Address, Shipping Address
  7. Please check product summary. Note: You may click on "Change my Cart" if you want to go back to the "Shopping Cart" if there will be changes on your order.
  8. Choose your Payment with various payment service providers to process your order.
  9. You must review and agree with Galleon Mercado Terms and Condition and Privacy Policy before you click on "Submit Order"
