  2. Via Online Banking: BPI, Chinabank, East West Bank, Metrobank, PBCom, PNB, PSBank, RCBC, Robinsons Bank, Security Bank, Sterling Bank, UCPB, Union Bank.
  3. Via Mobile App: AUB, BPI, Chinabank, Landbank, Maybank, Metrobank, PNB Savings Bank, UCPB, Unionbank, GCash, PayMaya

Note: Payment will be in prepaid in FULL prior to fulfillment of an order

Why is Cash Upon Delivery (COD) not acceptable as a payment option?
Cash Upon Delivery is not available for all transactions for Buyers protection. Galleon Mercado secures Buyers by holding money until the tracking shows that your order is delivered, this way Sellers are not able to scam Buyers. Also, Sellers ship items through their preferred Delivery Partners which we do not have control in Cash Upon Delivery (COD).

How to make a credit card/debit card payment?
You don't need to create a Paypal Account if paying with Credit Card/Debit Card. Please follow these step-by-step instructions:
  1. On the checkout page, choose "Paypal" as your payment method, then press â"Confirm Payment" You will be redirected to Paypal website.
  2. Press "Pay with a Bank Account or Credit Card" Button,
  3. Type in your Card details, Billing Address, Contact Information, and make sure to agree on Paypal's Privacy Statement, then
  4. Press "Pay Now" button
  5. Optional: After payment Press Button "Return to Merchant"
Note: No need to create a Paypal account if paying with credit/debit card. Subsidized Paypal Transaction Fee of 4.5% + Php 15.00 to be shouldered by the Buyer.

How to Pay with GCAsh/ PayMaya Virtual Card?
 
 For GCash Payment:
  1. Open the GCash App
  2. Select "Show More" and then select "Pay Online"
  3. Select Pay with American Express VirtualPay"
  4. Enter your Email Address
  5. Confirm by selecting "Okay"
  6. You will receive a confirmation sent to your email, and you can now enjoy using your GCash American Express Virtual Card to pay via Paypal, Credit Card, you may also use bank transfer option to deposit to our BDO account
 For PayMaya:
  1. Once you successfully register from PayMaya
  2. Open the PayMaya App to activate your virtual card
  3. Select "Online Payment Card Number"
  4. Type in your personal information
  5. You now have your very own PayMaya virtual card to pay via Paypal, Credit Card, you may also use bank transfer option to deposit to our BDO account.
