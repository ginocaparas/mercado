Restricted Items

While we are allowing Sellers to sell almost any items on Galleon Mercado, there are certain items that is restricted or banned from being listed on our site. It is important for the Sellers to keep this mind in order to avoid inconvenience. Among these products are:

  • Firearms, ammunition, and explosives
  • Firearm replicas (gas-powered airsoft guns as well as its parts and accessories)
  • Deadly weapons including, but not limited to swords, and knives
  • Drugs, narcotics, and abortion-inducing products
  • Pornographic materials
  • Narcotics, drugs and items for inducing unlawful abortions
  • Precious stones and metals and its alloys
  • Poisonous products and infectious substances
  • Animals and animal fur
  • Plants and wildlife
  • Human remains
  • Contaminated foods
  • Corrosives and gasses
  • Flammable items
  • Lottery and sweepstakes tickets
  • Currency
  • Counterfeit and pirated goods
  • Printed materials that advocate the overthrow of the government, urge forcible resistance against the law, or threaten to take the life or inflict bodily harm on any person in the Philippines.
